One site, 3 services

Accordion Abuse Entertaiment Website

Project overview

This website was meant to showcase a young venture where two people wanted to combine their businesses because of their love for Conjunto and Tejano music. That's how Accordion Abuse Entertainment came into being.

Role

Web designer

Duration

3 months

Context

Personal project for a small American company from the entertainment industry.

Goal

Creating a representative website for promotion 3 services: promotion, dj & radio, photography (light & dark mode)

Challenge 1

Combination of 3 services on one website & cultural barrier

Site structure

The first step was to design a site structure to combine all services in one logical world. To help create content for individual pages, I wrote down what I thought should be included on the pages.

After the talks,
it's time to get to work

It only took two meetings to agree on the structure, style, and direction we wanted. Since that was one of my first "commercial" projects, I started with establishing the grid and breakpoints for the layout. Then, I selected typography and colors. I designed UI components based on site architecture and an essential style guide.

Takeways

Unfortunately, the project ended at the high-fidelity stage. A prototype was also not prepared because the website's ordering parties needed to provide all the materials.

Nevertheless, this project taught me to cooperate in different time zones, specify precise questions, and not start a design from the high-fidelity phase because there will always be some changes during the design process and subsequent materials.

I also learned that well-designed components speed up subsequent work, and operating variables shorten the time needed to make corrections.

Aspects that were a challenge in this project:

Dark mode - ensuring readability when you have only black at your disposal is demanding, especially for text contrast and elevation of elements, but luckily, tools like the Figma plugin "Contrast" come to the rescue. And of course, every little change should be checked after applying.

Simple and logical structure thanks to which 3 different services will look uniform.

Did I achieve the Goal and cope with the Challenges?

The initial feedback shows that yes, the goal has been achieved. The website received a very good review from the company that ordered it.
