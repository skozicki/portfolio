Project overview

Connect for renovations. Finding local neighbors to help or join renovation projects with a web app based on a conversation with a potential user.

Platforms

Browser

Context

Privet concept of a product. The idea arose from the owners' interest in renovating old houses. During the conversations, it turned out that most of the work was done by acquaintances and friends. Why not extend it to your neighbors?

Goal

Designing an easy and transparent platform for the local community.

Challenge

USABILITY: Ensuring the tool is user-friendly and intuitive, so users can easily navigate.
SAFETY MATTERS: Letting strangers into our homes.
FINDING THE RIGHT FIT: Wasting time on someone who can't do anything.

Takeways

USABILITY

No unnecessary functionalities, simplified navigation, focusing on user presentation in order to create a trusted community.
Useful = usability + utility

SAFETY

This is a very complex issue because there can be thousands of reasons for possible "fear" of using this application. One of them could be no money evolve...interesting... To discover why they might be afraid to use this app, we may use surveys with potential users or face-to-face interviews. Because this is only a product concept, those weren't conducted.

FINDING THE RIGHT FIT

Providing the basic information is a must. To make it even more effective, I introduce a profile page to ensure more information and the possibility of contacting or writing a helpful review.
