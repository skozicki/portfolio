Print design

Designing for print is my first love and one I will never forget. The ability to touch the material, run your finger over the printed materials, and see it in different light conditions is a feeling that cannot be described in words. I transferred some of my skills gained in the print projects to the digital world, such as composition, typography, color theory, and awareness of the project's surroundings.
